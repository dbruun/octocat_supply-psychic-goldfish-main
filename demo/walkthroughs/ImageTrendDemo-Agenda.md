# ImageTrend Kickoff — Agenda

**Date:** May 4th, 2026 | **Duration:** 2 Hours | **Audience:** Engineering Leadership & Team Leads

> Each section presents a decision ImageTrend needs to make, walks through the options, and demonstrates the recommended approach live.

---

| # | Topic | Decision to Make | Time |
|---|-------|-----------------|------|
| — | **Copilot Basics Warm-Up** | *No decision — shared foundation.* Quick demos of ghost text, inline chat, ask & explain, quick fix, and commit generation so everyone sees what Copilot does out of the box. | 15 min |
| 1 | **Product Vision & Requirements** | Where do requirements live, and in what format? How do we write them so both humans *and* AI agents can act on them? | 10 min |
| 2 | **Repo & Architecture** | Monorepo vs. multi-repo? What architecture patterns do we standardize on so AI-generated code is consistent? Service bus now or later? | 10 min |
| 3 | **Coding Tool Selection** | Which AI coding platform do we standardize on — and does that lock us into one model? | 10 min |
| 4 | **Sub-Agents: Plan, Develop, Test** | How do we structure AI workflows so each SDLC phase has a purpose-built agent instead of one generic "write code" button? | 15 min |
| 5 | **AI Collaboration Protocols** | What are the rules of engagement when humans and AI agents write code simultaneously? Branch strategy, conflict prevention, review standards. | 10 min |
| 6 | **PR Review Rules** | What review process do AI-generated PRs go through, and how do we enforce it? (Spoiler: 2 human approvals, no exceptions.) | 10 min |
| 7 | **Version Control Everything** | How do we encode our coding standards and internal patterns so Copilot follows them — and how do we manage changes over time? | 10 min |
| 8 | **Prototyping & Requirements** | What's the fastest way to turn a rough idea into a working prototype stakeholders can validate — internally and externally? | 10 min |
| 9 | **Design System Ingestion** | How do we ensure every AI-generated component automatically follows our brand guidelines without manual correction? | 10 min |
| 10 | **CI/CD Toolchain & Deployment** | What pipeline does code go through before it ships — and how do we set it up so AI code passes the same gates as human code? | 10 min |
| 11 | **Test Agents & Automation** | What does our testing strategy look like when AI can generate, run, and self-heal tests autonomously? | 10 min |
| | | **Buffer for Q&A / transitions** | ~10 min |
| | | **Total** | **~130 min** |
